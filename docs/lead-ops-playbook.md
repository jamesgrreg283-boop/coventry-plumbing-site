# Lead Ops Playbook

## Response SLA

- Target first response in under 2 minutes for emergency leads.
- If first call is missed, trigger an SMS within 60 seconds.
- Retry call at +5 minutes and +15 minutes.

## Qualification Checklist

- Confirm issue type and urgency.
- Confirm decision maker (homeowner, tenant, landlord, business).
- Confirm exact postcode and access instructions.
- Confirm preferred contact method.

## Attribution Logging

- Keep UTM source, medium, campaign and landing page with each lead.
- Log call outcomes (answered, voicemail, no answer, booked).
- Review source-to-booking performance weekly.
